** ReadMe_NHIS.txt **

This folder contains the data and programs used to generate Table 1.1 in
Mastering Metrics. Table 1.1 presents descriptive statistics from the
2009 National Health Interview Survey (NHIS).

Data: 
* /NHIS/Data/NHIS2009_clean.dta

Code: 
* /NHIS/Code/NHIS2009_hicompare.do

Output: 
* hicompare_hsb.xml (columns 1-3 of Table 1.1)
* hicompare_wf.xml (columns 4-6 of Table 1.1)
